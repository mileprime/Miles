Chinedu Ochonogor ("Miles Prime")

Founder & CEO of MavenPay

My Journey and Inspiration

From the tender age of nine, I found myself mesmerized by the world of technology. My first encounter with Adobe Photoshop wasn't just about designing flyers; it was the spark that ignited a relentless passion to create, innovate, and transform. I realized early on that I wanted to do more than just manipulate images—I yearned to build technologies that could redefine industries and impact lives.

This burning desire led me to the university where I got admitted into the Electronics and Computer Engineering department, where I embarked on a path to study Computer Engineering. However, the conventional academic environment soon revealed its limitations. The curriculum lacked the depth and practical insights I sought to truly innovate and create impactful technology solutions. Determined to break free from these constraints, I made the courageous decision to leave college halfway through my program.

With unwavering support from my uncle, I ventured into the world of entrepreneurship, investing in cars to sustain myself while I delved into self-education. Online learning became my sanctuary, where I immersed myself in programming, exploring languages and frameworks that would lay the foundation for my future endeavors. This period of self-driven education was transformative, turning my initial fascination with design into a profound understanding of technology development.

In 2022, I took a monumental leap forward by moving to Dubai, a global epicenter of innovation and technology. This relocation was more than a change of scenery—it was a catalyst for growth. In Dubai, I immersed myself in advanced programming and cybersecurity studies, earning prestigious diplomas from Edoxi Training Institute Dubai in Certified Ethical Hacking, Computer Forensics and Hacking Investigator, and Full Stack Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). Additionally, at TeachMeCode Academy Dubai, I mastered PHP, MySQL, Laravel, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, solidifying my expertise as a Full Stack Developer.

Amidst this rapid skill acquisition, I confronted a profound challenge: the limited accessibility to reliable payment services as an African. This personal hurdle illuminated a glaring gap in the fintech landscape—a gap MavenPay was born to fill. MavenPay is not just a fintech solution; it's a visionary platform committed to providing seamless, secure, and globally accessible payment, travel, and tourism services. Inspired by global advancements in technology, MavenPay embodies my mission to bridge financial gaps and empower individuals and businesses worldwide.

Adopting the name "Miles Prime" symbolizes the apex of my journey—a blend of the miles traveled in pursuit of knowledge and the prime moment to lead transformative change. MavenPay stands as a testament to resilience, innovation, and the unwavering belief that technology can and should be accessible to all.
